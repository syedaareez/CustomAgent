# Requirements Summary

- BR count: 5
- FR count: 6
- NFR count: 5

## Top 3 Must Have items from BRD
1. BR-001: Present a concise professional summary with role/title, value proposition, top skills, and a prominent recruiter contact CTA.
2. BR-002: Use a recruiter-focused structure with distinct sections for headline, summary, experience highlights, strengths, personal story, and contact details.
3. BR-003: Ensure the page is mobile-first, responsive, and accessible with readable typography, sufficient contrast, and keyboard navigability.

## Top 3 Must Have items from FRD
1. FR-001: Recruiter-focused messaging that clearly communicates professional identity, key skills, and career goals within 5 seconds.
2. FR-002: Structured content sections with clear headings for summary, strengths, career highlights, and contact/call to action.
3. FR-006: Clear recruiter action prompt enabling easy access to contact or resume next steps.

## Top 3 Must Have items from NFR
1. NFR-001: Page load time of 2 seconds or less on a 4G mobile connection.
2. NFR-002: WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards compliance.
3. NFR-004: Responsive design across desktop, tablet, and mobile viewports without horizontal scrolling or unreadable text.
